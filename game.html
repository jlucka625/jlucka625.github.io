PAPERCUTS

>> Click here to play <<

Papercuts was created for the class CAP 3020 "Theory and Practice of Multimedia Production". It was a group project that consisted of four members and my primary role was the Technical Lead. The resposbilities of the Tech Lead position were to ensure everyone learned how to use the Unity Engine, delegate coding tasks, and write a majority of the scripts for the objects in the game. However, we were still required to participate in every aspect of developing the game. I also contributed to sound effects, models, textures, level designs, and developing the story.

Papercuts is a 2.5D Platformer created with the Unity Engine. The story follows an ill boy who dreams that he is in a paper world and must save his friends and family from the evil Paper Witch.

SUPER SLIME BLOB

>> Click here to download (pc only) <<

Super Slime Blob started off as a group project in high school. Originally, it was just a short demo with ten levels. After I graduated high school, I took the project into my own hands and turned it into a full game. I added more enemies, levels, and features. I created my own sound effects and sprites, and asked one of my friends to compose the bgm's.

Super Slime Blob was created with the Microsoft XNA Game Studio. It is a 2D Platformer about a pink blob that must save the world from the evil green slimes.


MUNCHER

coming to google play store soon

I started working on Muncher so that I could teach myself how to make games for mobile devices. Muncher was made with Cocos2d-x, so it also gave me additional C++ practice. The objective of the game is to catch as many candies as you can. If you accidently miss or eat too many fruits and veggies, then you lose.
