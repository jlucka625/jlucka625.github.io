EVENT POSTER


Hallowhoops is an annual event that takes place at the Lakeside Residential Complex at the University of Florida. It is a haunted house run by residents in the complex and gets the name "Hallowhoops" because it always takes place the day after Halloween ("whoops" we missed Halloween). The theme this year was classic horror movie villains: Michael, Jason, Freddy, and Ghostface. I chose this event for my project because it is something that I am a part of every year, and I wanted to take advantage of doing my project and helping for the event at the same time. The final poster was used as publicity for the event.

DESIGN THINKING


This was a group project for the non-majors graphic design class at the University of Florida. The goal of the project was to use the design thinking process to come up with a product solves a problem for the student body and/or local community. The problem we found was the accessibility of coffee on campus, particularly in terms of the amount of coffee locations and the amount of time it takes to get coffee. Our solution was "The Bean Machine", a high-tech coffee vending machine that would be installed in various places around campus. My primary contributions to the project were the logo, and the design of the machine itself.

PERSONAL BRANDING


This project involved creating a professional logotype, resume, letterhead, and business card. For the logotype, we were required to type our names in at least 20 different fonts and recieve feedback for the top three. We then experimented with alignment, size, position, color, etc for our names in those three fonts. Three of those experimental logotypes were submitted for critique and the feedback was used to create the final logotype. The resume building process started with a research phase involving finding resume designs online and analyzing what we liked/didn't like. After two iterations, the final resume was created. Address and phone number have been edited in these pdf versions for privacy reasons.

NATIONAL AGRI-MARKETING ASSOCIATION (NAMA)


In early Janurary I was requested to do logo design for UF NAMA. NAMA is a nationwide competition where students must form a team and market an agricultural product. Their original idea was "Red Diamonds", a premium strawberry, and it was requested to be cutesy and over-the-top. The team later decided to change the look to a more sophisticated feel, which is shown in the second design. Finally, the team decided to overhaul the Red Diamonds idea and market "Rosies". Rosies were meant to be marketed to children and families in the same style as Halos.
