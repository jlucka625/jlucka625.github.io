BACKGROUND


Papercuts was developed by Team DigiSquid using Unity 3D in 2013 for CAP3020 Theory and Practice of Mutlimedia Production. As technical lead, my primary responsibility was to delegate programming tasks to myself and my team members and make sure the technical side of development went smoothly. All four team members were required to help with each aspect of the game, meaning I also had a part in desinging the game, making some 3D models/artwork, and creating sound effects.

The game is about an ill boy named Ripley who spends his free time playing with his paper creation - Rip, a boy with a cardboard box for a head. At a critical point in Ripley's illness, he dreams that he is in a paper world and that he has taken the form of Rip. The evil Paper Witch seeks to turn his dream into a nightmare when she kidnaps his friends and family, and it is up to Rip to stop her.
